Painting Workshops in Prague

It’s time to learn the basics of painting while sipping on some wine in the magical city of Prague. Our practical step-by-step workshops will encourage you to create your own artwork to take home in just a few hours. Not an artist? No problem. We have practice rounds to get you comfortable before diving into the real thing.

A warm experience worth sharing with friends, colleagues and even alone to let your inner artist shine. All materials and superhero capes are provided.
